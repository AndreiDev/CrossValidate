MVPtest FAQ

Why do I get the message telling me that the intersection group is empty?
The opinion-leaders you choose must have common followers/following, otherwise it is impossible to find an intersecting group. Choose opinion-leaders that are somewhat connected.
Why do I get zero "early-adopters"?
That means that the intersection group created by intersecting the followers and the following of your opinion-leaders (according to your choice) is not empty, however all the early-adopters are filtered out by the default filtering parameters. Try to soften to filters or choose another, more connected, opinion-leaders.
Why do I have to wait 15 minutes between each MVPtest?
Twitter API allows a 15 minute window per user. Thus, after each MVPtest, you will have to wait 15 minuters for Twitter to reset your API usage.
What is "Number of fetches exceeded"?
That means you are trying to intersect huge groups of followers and following (or that your account is rich in followers or following). Currently, MVPtest is designed for relativly new accounts, so if you have an account with large following or followers numbers (more then 5,000), your usage of MVPtest will be less effective. Try changing the intersection parameters to avoid intersection with large groups (more then 25,000).
How exactly do you follow the "early-adopters"?
MVPtest starts to follow the "early adopters" after you choose them. Currently, it follows one of them every 30 minutes in order to prevent bulk-following which is prohibited by Twitter.
