Rapid validation of start-up ideas in target markets (AKA smoke testing)

When you are ready click below to start your MVPtest

Before you begin...

Know thyself

Create a short and focused pitch of the idea you want to validate. Look around the arena to see who are the opinion-leaders in the field your idea is addressing. Preferably, create a simple landing page for your value proposition.

Twitter account

The twitter account should be specificaly tailored for your idea. The description part should include your best pitch on the subject and the link to your landing page. Please, never forget to include a profile image.

Two key players

Investigate to find out who are the opinion-leaders in your target market. These people will be followed by tweeters you are targeting. Grab the Twitter usernames of those two opinion-leaders and you're ready to roll.

From opinion-leaders. To early-adopters.

Give MVPtest the twitter usernames of two opinion-leaders in some area of interest, and it will give you a group of tweeters that is the intersection between the following and followers of both users you gave. That group will form the basis for you MVPtest.

You choose. MVPtest follows.

MVPtest makes it easy for you to laser-focus on your target market using a set of simple Twitter accosiated parameters; then MVPtest follows your early-adopters for you, projecting you the progress of follow-backs.

Set actionable metrics. Lean Startup way.

The metric you define with MVPtest is the percetage of people following you back, thus finding your idea appealing and interesting. Set the timeframe for the validation and decide what follow-back percantage will constitute a successful validation. MVPtest will use these to monitor you progress and overall validation outcome.
